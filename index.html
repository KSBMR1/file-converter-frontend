Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service completely free?

Yes, all our basic conversion tools are completely free to use. We offer premium options for high-volume users or those needing advanced features.

How is my data security ensured?

All uploaded files are encrypted during transmission. We automatically delete all files from our servers after 30 minutes, and no file content is ever accessed by humans.

What's the maximum file size for conversion?

Free users can convert files up to 100MB in size. Premium users get increased limits up to 500MB per file.

Will my document formatting be preserved?

We strive to maintain the original formatting as closely as possible. Complex layouts, custom fonts, or specialized formatting may have minor differences in the converted file.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Yes, free users can convert up to 3 files simultaneously. Premium users enjoy batch conversion for up to 50 files at once.

Do I need to install any software?

No, our service is entirely web-based. You only need a modern web browser to use all our conversion tools.
